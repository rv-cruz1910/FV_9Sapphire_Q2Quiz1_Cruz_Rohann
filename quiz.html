SHIB


SHIB or Shiba Inu is an Etherium-based token that serves as an alternative for the memecoin "Dogecoin" (DOGE), created in August, 2020. It is inspired by the dog breed Shiba Inu, and was created by an anonymous group of people/a person that goes by the pseudonym "Ryoshi".
